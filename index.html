What we do

Kulina's mission is to "serve the best meal with an outstanding customer experience at the lowests possible price every single day". We want to be customer's default meal option by prividing quality product with on-time delivery. We help customers save time and modeny for each meal they get from us, so they can better spend it on other things that matter

We took pride in being the first food tech startup to join " Google launchpad Accelerator " in San Francisco, and the only one from Indonesia in Winter 2018. Our startup is backed by the same investor who invested in Ele.me (China's unicorn foos delivery startup)

Join us in our misson to delivery best meals to ten millions of Jakartans. Hurry! be part of our Kulinas familiy
